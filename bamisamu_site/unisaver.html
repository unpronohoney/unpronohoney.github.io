UniSaver

University GPA Calculator App

What is UniSaver?

UniSaver was developed to prevent students from being confused by how their GPA will be affected by their courses.

Users can calculate their GPA by entering the course information one by one, or after a little bit course information they can get GPA scenarios calculated by UniSaver, or they can skip the course entry process with E-Devlet (Turkish Government) transcript documents and calculate GPA with all courses listed on their transcripts.
